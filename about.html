Our Story

Tiptoe Shoes began in Australia during the early 1990s with a mission to create footwear that blends comfort, quality, and everyday practicality. We started as a small business focused on designing shoes that meet the needs of active Australians.

Over the years, our collection has grown to include walking shoes, casual sneakers, and versatile everyday footwear. Our goal has always been to combine functionality with modern design, ensuring every pair supports an active lifestyle without compromising style.

Our Philosophy

We believe that footwear should empower movement and feel great all day. At Tiptoe Shoes, we focus on:

  • Creating shoes that prioritize comfort and support
  • Using high-quality materials for lasting durability
  • Incorporating sustainable practices wherever possible
  • Designing shoes suited to the diverse Australian lifestyle

Our mission is to help people move with confidence, comfort, and ease every day.

Meet Our Team

Our team is made up of talented designers, skilled product developers, and dedicated customer service professionals. Each member contributes to creating shoes that meet our high standards of comfort, quality, and style.

From concept to production, we carefully test every design, and customer feedback plays a key role in improving our products and innovating for the future.
