Our Story

Tiptoe Shoes began in Australia during the early 1990s with a mission to create footwear that blends comfort, quality, and everyday practicality.

Over the years, our collection has grown to include walking shoes, casual sneakers, and versatile everyday footwear.

Our Philosophy

We believe that footwear should empower movement and feel great all day.

  • Creating shoes that prioritize comfort and support
  • Using high-quality materials for lasting durability
  • Incorporating sustainable practices wherever possible
  • Designing shoes suited to the diverse Australian lifestyle

Meet Our Team

Our team is made up of talented designers, skilled product developers, and dedicated customer service professionals.
