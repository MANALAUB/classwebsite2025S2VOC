Our History

Tiptoe Shoes was established in Australia in the early 1990s with a simple goal â€” to create comfortable and reliable footwear designed for everyday life. The brand was built around the needs of people who are constantly on the move, whether at work, at home, or outdoors.

Beginning as a small local business, Tiptoe Shoes focused on designing footwear that prioritised comfort, stability, and durability. Over time, the brand expanded its range to include walking shoes, casual footwear, and supportive everyday styles.

Today, Tiptoe Shoes remains committed to designing footwear suited to Australian conditions, combining practical design with modern styling to support active lifestyles.

Our Vision

Our vision at Tiptoe Shoes is to support comfortable movement at every stage of the day. We believe footwear should enhance mobility while still looking stylish and modern.

We aim to achieve this by:

  • Designing footwear that provides comfort and support
  • Using quality materials that improve durability
  • Considering sustainability in our production processes
  • Creating designs suitable for Australian lifestyles

At Tiptoe Shoes, we focus on creating footwear that allows customers to move with confidence, comfort, and ease.

Our Team

The Tiptoe Shoes team consists of designers, product developers, and customer service professionals who share a commitment to quality footwear. Our team works collaboratively to ensure every product meets high standards of comfort and performance.

From early design concepts to final product testing, each stage of development is carefully reviewed. Customer feedback plays an important role in shaping new designs and improving existing collections.
